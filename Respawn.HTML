RESPAWN

Developed for a school project, the goal was to make a Synthwave style game. I chose a first person wave shooter in Unreal Engine 5 using PS1‑style graphics. Researching the PS1, I learned about vertex snapping, caused by integer‑based vertex positions, which made objects shift when the camera moved. This is why many PS1 games used fixed camera angles or avoided free first‑person movement.
